About Us

Empowering Students to Reach Their Full Potential

MT 6V33 Educational Hub Ltd is a premier educational consultancy based in Lagos, Nigeria. We specialize in guiding students through every step of their academic journey — from university admissions and scholarship applications to standardized test preparation and study abroad programs.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between ambition and achievement, ensuring every student gets the opportunity to study at their dream institution, both locally and internationally.

Our Services

Comprehensive Educational Solutions

From admission guidance to visa processing, we provide end-to-end support for your academic success

Student Recruitment

Expert recruitment for admission into tertiary institutions both locally in Nigeria and internationally across top global universities.

Guidance & Counseling

Professional counseling for students seeking admission into universities, polytechnics, and other higher institutions of learning.

Application Processing

Assistance in preparation and processing of admission applications, including personal statements, recommendation letters, and required documents.

Career & Academic Advisory

Expert career counseling and academic advisory services to students and parents in selecting appropriate courses and institutions.

Scholarship & Financial Aid

Connecting students with available scholarship opportunities, grants, and financial aid programs both within and outside Nigeria.

Seminars & Workshops

Organizing educational seminars, workshops, and training programs on career development, admission requirements, and study strategies.

Standardized Test Prep

Assistance in preparation and registration for JAMB, WAEC, NECO, TOEFL, IELTS, SAT, GRE, GMAT, and other relevant examinations.

Institutional Collaboration

Collaboration with tertiary institutions, educational bodies, and government agencies in facilitating student admission and academic placement.

Study Abroad Visa Guide

Guidance on visa application procedures and related documentation for study abroad programs (excluding tour and travel services).

Mentorship & Coaching

Mentorship and academic coaching services to help students improve their academic performance and enhance their chances of admission.

Credential Evaluation

Assistance in the evaluation and verification of academic credentials for students applying to foreign institutions.

Sponsorship Initiatives

Partnership with foundations, corporate organizations, and government agencies in creating scholarship programs and sponsorship initiatives.
